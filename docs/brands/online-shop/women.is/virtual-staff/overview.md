# women.is Virtual Staff Overview

> **NOTE**: This document defines Virtual Staff roles (AI), not human employees.

## Purpose

- Define virtual staff roles specific to women.is operations
- Establish how AI assistance supports the online shop business
- Document integration with global virtual staff leadership

## Brand-Specific Virtual Staff

### Marketing Assistant

- Supports marketing campaigns and content creation
- Manages social media presence and engagement
- Analyzes marketing performance and trends

### Customer Support Assistant

- Handles customer inquiries and support tickets
- Provides product information and recommendations
- Manages order status inquiries and basic troubleshooting

### Product Research Assistant

- Researches trending products and market opportunities
- Analyzes competitor offerings and pricing
- Supports product selection and inventory decisions

## Coordination with Global Staff

- Reports to Operations Manager on operational matters
- Works with Accountant on sales and expense tracking
- Coordinates with Policy Manager on compliance
- Provides data to Archivist for documentation
