# lisadigiglio.com Brand Profile

## Brand Identity

- Professional photography services brand
- Specializing in portrait, event, and commercial photography
- Personal brand built on artistic vision and client relationships
- Target market: Individuals, families, and businesses seeking quality photography

## Brand Values

- Artistic excellence and creative vision
- Authentic storytelling through imagery
- Client collaboration and satisfaction
- Professional service and reliability
- Continuous learning and technique refinement

## Brand Voice

- Professional yet warm and personable
- Creative and artistic without being pretentious
- Clear communication about services and expectations
- Encouraging and supportive during shoots

## Visual Identity

- Photography portfolio showcasing signature style
- Website design reflecting aesthetic sensibility
- Logo and branding consistent with artistic vision
- Color palette and typography that complement photography work
