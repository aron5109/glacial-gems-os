# lisadigiglio.com Overview

## Business Model

- Professional photography services provider
- Session-based and package-based pricing
- Digital and print product offerings
- Recurring client relationships and referrals

## Service Offerings

- Portrait photography (individuals, families, headshots)
- Event photography (weddings, corporate events, parties)
- Commercial photography (products, business, real estate)
- Photo editing and retouching services
- Print products and albums

## Market Position

- Premium quality photography services
- Personal attention and customized approach
- Building reputation through portfolio and referrals
- Differentiation through artistic style and client experience

## Key Objectives

- Build strong portfolio and online presence
- Maintain high client satisfaction and referral rates
- Optimize booking and workflow efficiency
- Achieve sustainable revenue growth
- Develop signature style and market recognition

## Success Metrics

- Number of bookings per month
- Average booking value
- Client satisfaction and referral rates
- Portfolio quality and diversity
- Social media engagement and reach
