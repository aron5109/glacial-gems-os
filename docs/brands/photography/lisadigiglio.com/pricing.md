# lisadigiglio.com Pricing Strategy

## Pricing Philosophy

- Value-based pricing reflecting expertise and quality
- Transparent pricing with clear package structures
- Premium positioning aligned with service quality
- Flexible options for different client budgets

## Package Structure

- Session fee covering time and talent
- Tiered packages with varying deliverable counts
- À la carte options for prints and products
- Add-on services (extra time, locations, rush editing)

## Service Pricing

- Portrait sessions: Standard, extended, and premium options
- Event coverage: Hourly rates and full-day packages
- Commercial work: Project-based or day-rate pricing
- Editing and retouching: Per-image or package rates

## Payment Terms

- Deposit required to secure booking
- Payment schedule for larger projects
- Accepted payment methods
- Cancellation and rescheduling policies
- Late payment terms and fees

## Competitive Positioning

- Premium pricing tier in local market
- Justification through quality and experience
- Value proposition beyond price alone
- Strategic use of mini-sessions and promotions
