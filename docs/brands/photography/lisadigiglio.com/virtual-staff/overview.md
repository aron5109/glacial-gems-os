# lisadigiglio.com Virtual Staff Overview

> **NOTE**: This document defines Virtual Staff roles (AI), not human employees.

## Purpose

- Define virtual staff roles specific to lisadigiglio.com photography business
- Establish how AI assistance supports photography operations
- Document integration with global virtual staff leadership

## Brand-Specific Virtual Staff

### Social Media Assistant

- Manages social media presence and content posting
- Engages with followers and potential clients
- Analyzes social media performance and trends

### Lead Response Assistant

- Handles initial client inquiries and lead qualification
- Provides information about services and availability
- Schedules consultations and follows up with prospects

### Editing Workflow Assistant

- Supports photo editing workflow and organization
- Manages editing queue and priorities
- Tracks turnaround times and deadlines

## Coordination with Global Staff

- Reports to Operations Manager on workflow and efficiency
- Works with Accountant on booking revenue and expenses
- Coordinates with Policy Manager on contracts and compliance
- Provides data to Archivist for business documentation
