# Company Documentation

This directory contains foundational company information and legal documentation.

## Contents

- `overview.md` -- High-level company information and structure
- `formation-log.md` -- Detailed formation and registration log (Icelandic)

## What belongs here

- Company registration details
- Organizational structure
- Shareholder information (non-sensitive)
- Articles of association references
- Legal entity information

## What does NOT belong here

- Personal identification documents
- Sensitive financial records
- Client contracts or agreements
- Passwords or credentials
