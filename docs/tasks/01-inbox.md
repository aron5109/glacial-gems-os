---

## Task Format

Each task should follow this structure:

- [ ] Task title
  - Description:
  - Proposed by:
  - Priority: (Low / Medium / High)
  - Status: (Open / In Progress / Waiting / Done)
  - Owner: (Human only)

---

## Rules

- AI may suggest tasks.
- AI may draft task descriptions.
- AI may not mark tasks as Done.
- Only humans assign ownership.
- Only humans close tasks.
- Major changes require human review.
