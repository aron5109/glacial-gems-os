# Virtual Staff Overview

> **NOTE**: This document defines Virtual Staff roles (AI), not human employees.

## Purpose

- Define the concept and role of virtual staff in Glacial Gems OS
- Establish guidelines for AI-powered assistance across the organization
- Document the integration of virtual staff with human workflows

## Virtual Staff Concept

- Virtual staff are AI-powered assistants designed to support specific business functions
- Each virtual staff member has a defined role, responsibilities, and decision-making scope
- They operate within established policies and report to human oversight

## Benefits

- Consistent execution of standard operating procedures
- 24/7 availability for routine tasks and queries
- Scalable support without proportional cost increases
- Documented and traceable decision-making processes

## Integration Guidelines

- Virtual staff complement human team members, not replace them
- All virtual staff actions should be auditable and transparent
- Critical decisions require human approval
- Regular review and optimization of virtual staff performance
